ABOUT US

Marie Pense Center (MPC) provides intervention services to children in their home and community settings including: preschools, hospitals, homes, and day care centers.

Our goal is to meet the needs of every individual and family that we have the opportunity to work with. We aim to do this in a creative, holistic, caring, and empathetic way.

MPC Mission

To employ respect, thoughtfulness, patience, and an open mind in every interaction we have with children, adolescents, adults, and families.
